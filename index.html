Konstanz on the Bodensee

Welcome

Konstanz is a charming lakeside destination in southern Germany, set on the shores of Lake Constance at the Swiss border. Renowned for its beautifully preserved medieval old town, vibrant waterfront, and relaxed atmosphere, the city offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Visitors can stroll through cobbled streets lined with historic buildings, enjoy lakeside cafés and gardens, explore museums and local markets, or take scenic boat cruises across the lake to neighboring Germany, Switzerland, and Austria. With its mild climate, cycling and walking trails, and easy access to the Alps, Konstanz is an ideal destination for travelers seeking a mix of sightseeing, outdoor adventure, and leisurely lakeside living.
